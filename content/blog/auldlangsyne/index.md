> And so, with that crisp, whipcrack sound, it all began . . . I knew I’d shattered the balance of the day, the spacious calm of this beach on which I had been happy. But I fired four shots more into the inert body, on which they left no visible trace. And each successive shot was another loud, fateful rap on the door of my undoing.

-- Meursault, The Stranger, Albert Camus

And thus commences a new, possibly subconsciously awaited, chapter in my life. I'll be spending the upcoming weeks studying up on the fundamental ideas behind machine learning - a field which, in the last three or so years, seems to have taken the world by storm. Simultaneously, throughout the next few weeks, I will be implementing the models that I learn about and write about them here on this blog, and probably write about some unrelated things as well now that I am no longer legally obliged to keep mum.
